About Me

My name is Jedidiah Kolo, a dedicated development professional, creative designer, and aspiring technology specialist with a passion for creating meaningful impact. I currently work with the Women Initiative for Sustainable Environment (WISE), where I contribute to community development, environmental sustainability, and women's empowerment initiatives. Through my work, I have developed strong skills in communication, project coordination, social media management, stock keeping, and community engagement.

I am passionate about continuous learning and believe that knowledge is one of the greatest tools for personal and societal transformation. My background in Agricultural Bio-Environmental Engineering Technology, combined with my growing interest in cybersecurity and front-end development, reflects my desire to bridge environmental sustainability with innovative technology. I enjoy solving problems, learning new skills, and using creativity to design solutions that make information accessible and engaging.

Beyond my professional life, I am a cheerful, dependable, and people-oriented individual who values integrity, excellence, and service. I enjoy graphic design, photography, music, and collaborating with others to achieve shared goals. My long-term vision is to become a highly skilled cybersecurity professional while continuing to contribute to initiatives that empower communities, inspire positive change, and create lasting impact across Nigeria and beyond.
